About Me

Hello Friends!
My name is Kenzi (pronounced Ken-Ji)
I'm 23 years old, living in Brooklyn, and I'm so glad you're here!
Welcome to My Mind


The things I care about most are my friends, music, homemade food, and preserving the natural beauty of our Earth. I value honest conversation, feeling your feelings, and a really good cookie. I'm a data analyst by day, which is cool, but it doesn't define me. I consider myself shy, but friendly if given enough time to figure out what I want to say. One day I hope to be as open and honest in person as I am on here. The things written and seen here are my own thoughts, feelings, and experiences. It's basically a manifestation of all the words I can't seem to find when I want to express myself in person.

I hope my website can be comforting, relatable, or at the least, amusing. Enjoy!
