Thoughts and Feelings

KPOP Journey

"The sense of “other” is emphasized in the use of the “K” in K-Pop. In the United States, it would be fair to say that pop music would be defined as all mainstream, popular music. But the distinction here is that “mainstream” music is largely in reference to western, English-speaking countries and not inclusive of global pop music –if such a thing even exists. "

Baking My Feelings

"Amongst the chaos of everyday life, it feels good to know I can take these raw ingredients that don’t really mean anything individually, and turn them into something beautiful (sometimes) and delicious (most of the time). And even in those times where the end product is not beautiful or delicious, it feels good to at least try and make sense of it all"

When Did This Get Here?

"Nothing was being discovered or observed, the routes were planned, and the destination was already known. So much of a city I have spent so much of my life in ignored, overlooked, seen, but not noticed."

Boredom and Muffins

" I’m frustrated without a challenge, but I’m also too tired to take on anything challenging. Sometimes it feels easier to admit defeat and remain restless. I don’t think I feel bored or restless because there is nothing to do. It’s probably the opposite if anything. There are so many options to choose from, and it’s so hard to decide on a course of action that it’s easier to just be bored and do something mindless. Spending all my energy trying to ignore these feelings, I prevent myself from getting to the root of my restlessness."

Cookies

"There are so many ways to express yourself, and so many places to travel to, and so many people to meet, and I just don’t understand how to do it all. I understand that it’s not really feasible to do everything and not be exhausted, but how do we choose what to pursue?"
